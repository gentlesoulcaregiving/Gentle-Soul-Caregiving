About Us

At Gentle Soul Caregiving, we are dedicated to providing exceptional care and support to seniors and individuals with disabilities. Our team of compassionate and experienced caregivers is committed to delivering personalized care that meets the unique needs of each client.

Our History

Gentle Soul Caregiving was founded by Tanya McDonald, a dedicated and experienced caregiver who saw a need for high-quality, personalized care in her community. With a passion for helping others, Tanya built a team of like-minded individuals who share her commitment to delivering exceptional care.

Our Mission and Values

Our mission is to provide compassionate and personalized care that enhances the quality of life for our clients. We value:

  • Compassion and empathy
  • Personalized care
  • Trust and integrity
  • Quality and excellence
  • Customer satisfaction
